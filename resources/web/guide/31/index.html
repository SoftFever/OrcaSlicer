In the 3D Printing community, we learn from each other’s successes and failures to adjust our own slicing parameters and settings. Orca Slicer follows the same principle and uses machine learning to improve its performance from the successes and failures of the vast number of prints by our users. We are training Orca Slicer to be smarter by feeding them the real-world data.
